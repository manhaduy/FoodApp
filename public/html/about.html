Description

This is an application for users to search for restaurants, see the menu and leave reviews. Users can register and login with a unique password. The user profile contains a list of the userâ€™s favorite restaurants that they have bookmarked. Users can also view their activity history. Other users can also rate comments made by other users through (like, dislike) and the top comment will be filtered out based on the number of likes. Restaurant owners can also upload their restaurant information like address, location, phone number and menu selection with food image (name, phone are unique for each restaurant).
